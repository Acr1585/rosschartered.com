Businesses and entrepreneurs

Ross Chartered provides practical counsel to growing businesses and ventures through encrypted email, messaging, and file storage.

Services

  • General transactional counsel relating to business development
  • Litigation or complex-litigation management
  • Alternative dispute resolution
  • Policy advocacy and agency
  • Business planning and entity formation
  • Intellectual property consulting (trademarks, copyrights, patents)
  • Financial modeling and budgeting
  • Prepare and draft agreements, typically relating to:
    • Licensing
    • Employment
    • Confidentiality
    • Competition
    • Waivers
    • Terms of Service

Retainers

Transactional

Ross Chartered aims to build long-term relationships with growing businesses. Prospective clients should expect to retain the firm for at least 3 months:

  • Starting: $500 for 2 hours per month
  • Moving: $1000 for 5 hours per month
  • Operating: $1500 for 8 hours per month
Litigation

Businesses that seek litigation support should contact Ross Chartered for specific counsel. The firm maintains a strong toolkit and solid network to address many issues in court.

  • Monthly reatainers with hourly fees
  • Contingency arrangements are available in some cases

Ross Chartered normally counsels the pursuit of alternative dispute resolutions.

“Discourage litigation. Persuade your neighbors to compromise whenever you can. As a peacemaker, the lawyer has superior opportunity of being a good man. There will still be business enough.”

Abraham Lincoln
Project-based Counsel

Ross Chartered also works on independent projects. The firm regularly prepares and drafts non-disclosure agreements for businesses in wide-ranging industries for a fixed fee. Ross Chartered also welcomes clients who seek professional advice to complete their projects.
